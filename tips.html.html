Top Travel Tips

  • Pack light but smart — prioritize essentials and versatile clothing.
  • Always keep digital and printed copies of important documents.
  • Learn a few basic words of the local language — it goes a long way!
  • Use apps like Google Maps, XE Currency, and Duolingo on the go.
  • Travel insurance is worth it — better safe than sorry.
  • Respect local cultures, customs, and dress codes.
  • Wake up early to avoid crowds and enjoy peaceful sightseeing.
